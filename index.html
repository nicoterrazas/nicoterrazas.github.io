Currently I am studying mechanical engineering at Rice University. I am the Historian for Society of Hispanic and Professional Engineers (SHPE) at Rice. I am enrolled in a Materials Science and Engineering Mechanics course, each of which are quite interesting to me, being the first few classes actually geared towards my major.

In August of 2017 I started working with Fresh Prints, a custom apparel printing company. I serve as Rice University's Campus Manager, working with organizations on campus to create some amazing designs and produce sales for my company.

My passion for photography continues as I take pictures at many events on campus and around. As the Historian for not only SHPE, but also for my residential college, Will Rice, I have multiple events each week for which I will be the photographer. In addition, I attempt to make time to take pictures for fun whenever I get the chance.
